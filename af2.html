01 // AIR SUPERIORITY FLEET

Dassault Rafale

India's Dassault Rafale program involves the Indian Air Force (IAF) and Navy, with the IAF receiving 36 jets and the Navy ordering 26 carrier-based Rafale Ms, deliveries beginning 2028

TOP SPEED: MACH 1.8

Sukhoi Su-30MKI

The Sukhoi Su-30MKI is a twin-seater, twin-engine, multi-role air superiority fighter developed by Russia and built under license in India by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited for the Indian Air Force.

TECH SPEED: MACH 2.0

HAL Tejas

The HAL Tejas is India's indigenously developed, light, multirole fighter jet, crucial for Make in India and boosting self-reliance in defense.

TOP SPEED: MACH 1.8

C-17 Globemaster III

The C-17A Globemaster III provides the Air Force with an unprecedented capacity for strategic airlift.

Purpose: Transport

02 // RADAR & GROUND SYSTEMS

NETRA (AEW&C)

An "Eye in the Sky" plane that sees enemy aircraft from hundreds of kilometers away.

Mobile Radar units

Heavy trucks that carry giant rotating radars to find hidden targets behind mountains.

Refueller Planes

Giant "Gas Stations" in the sky that fill fighter jets with fuel while they are flying.

03 // GARUD COMMANDO FORCE

The Garud Commando Force is the elite special forces unit of the IAF. They are trained to protect air bases, rescue pilots behind enemy lines, and help during big disasters.

> MOTTO: "Defense by Offence"

> SPECIALTY: Airfield Security & Combat Search/Rescue
