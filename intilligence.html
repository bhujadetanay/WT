Research and Analysis Wing: India's Silent Protectors

Who are they?

The Research and Analysis Wing (R.A.W.) is a team of very brave people who help keep India safe. They work quietly and don't tell anyone about their secret missions!

Special Missions

Operation Watch

Keeping a close eye on the borders to make sure everyone is safe at home.

The Sky Team

Using planes and satellites to see things from very high up in the sky.

Cyber Guard

Protecting computers from bad guys who want to steal secret information.

Invisible Cameras

Secret Radios

How to Become a Hero?

  • Study Hard: Learning different languages is a superpower!
  • Be Brave: Always stand up for what is right.
  • Keep Secrets: Don't tell your missions to anyone!
  • Stay Fit: A strong body helps a strong mind.
